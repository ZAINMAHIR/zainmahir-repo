About GreenEnergy

GreenEnergy Consultancy is a firm specialising in Energy services. As the largest global Green Energy sustainability consultancy Company, we partner with the world’s leading organizations, creating innovative solutions to sustainability challenges and unlocking commercial opportunities that meet the needs of today while preserving Our environment through green energy solutions for future generations. For us it is advising, stimulating and challenging, with the aim of making the company, or the project, stronger. Our strength lies mainly in my technological background and general knowledge is to optimize company’s energy planning, in the short and long term.

This includes:

  • Clarify and quantify strategies
  • Helping to map pure energy in geo-regions
  • Advising on optimal green energy management
  • If desired, functioning as an intermediary towards third parties

  • GreenEnergy (English for "Green Energy") is a comapny that focuses on energy that is envirnmental friendly, sustainable and the consumes less of the enviromental resources while supplying the much needed energy. We want to help making the planet greener and sustainable. Whether it concerns techniques (solar panels, wind turbines, batteries, ...) or sustainable strategies. We just want to help make it happen. There is only one globe and space travel is not for us...
  • Tell us! If you have a problem and you think we can help, contact us! Let's share knowledge! Dynamic discussion (and learning) is always fun and gives energy when it happens openly and with mutual respect.

Our Specialisations

Green energy

Sun & wind, battery, heat exchangers... We love it! But from the financial side (profit/loss, payback periods, ...). We can talk about yields, inverters and energy curves, please dont ask us to cover the technical side. We on our side we make sure that an investment decision can be made.

Green Financing techniques services

Green financing is to increase level of financial flows (from banking, micro-credit, insurance and investment) from the public, private and not-for-profit sectors to sustainable development priorities.. The most important thing remains customisation, tailored towards the needs of the company: What is the goal and how do we achieve it? We are also happy to help you with questions about other matters (KYC, hedging, green payment structures).

Corporate sustainability & climate change

From climate change to human rights, there is increasing urgency in company boardrooms to address the complex sustainability challenges facing the world. Investments in environment, health and safety (EHS) and sustainability programs are no exception - every dollar spent needs to protect or create value for the business.

Low carbon economy transition

The risks associated with a changing climate are recognized as systemic to the global economy. Pressure is increasing from investors, regulators, customers and society for business and governments to transition to a lower carbon economy. Advising at corporate and asset levels to identify and reduce carbon emissions and climate-related financial risk within the value chain.

What do WE stand for?

Our diverse team of world-class experts supports clients across the breadth of their organizations to operationalize sustainability, underpinned by our deep technical expertise in addressing their environmental, health, safety, risk, and social issues. We call this capability our “boots to boardroom” approach for its comprehensive service model that allows GreenEnergy to develop strategic and technical solutions that advance objectives on the ground or at the executive level.

  • Starting Energy companies often need to test and refine ideas, moving from an idea to a fundable and feasible plan. Here, we focus mainly on quantifying and calculating operating costs and investments, in order to lay a solid foundation for further growth. This is both to protect its own invested capital and to provide opportunities to attract external funding.

  • We always thought it was great assisting young entrepreneurs and helping them launch projects, especially those that are environmentally sustainable and friendly and since first investments are the hardest!

  • Sustainable Energy Growth companies with their dynamic environment and inexhaustible optimism sometimes also experience growing pains, whereby it is important not to put the elephant in your mouth all at once, but to eat it bite by bite.

    With these type of companies, it is important to help determine priorities, including whether short-term hyper-growth is sustainable in the long term. This is both for the shareholders (increase or dilute?) as well as the external financing parties (can they or do they want to follow?). Both towards risks taken (every risk has its price, but is the price justified?) and the new opportunities at hand.


  • Mature companies do they exist? In theory, yes, but every company knows better than to take it easy! If not, the decline has probably already begun. It is therefore a question of building on and protecting what is good and making plans to embark on a new growth plan. This can be done organically through own development (within or outside the company), or by skipping the start-up phase through an acquisition. Especially when it comes to reinventing themselves, companies need support, for sure when it comes to takeovers. The bride of course dresses as beautifully as possible, her parents (bankers, consultants) will especially highlight the good qualities (several layers even!), but it comes down to determining the right dowry. You don't want to deal with the winner's curse! And this is where an independent advisor can help you a lot!
  • For us, it is always fun to challenge these kinds of companies and help strengthen their plans! Sometimes that means building in safeguards and pointing out unforeseen risks. But always focused on the end goal and grafted onto the identity of the company.
